About the Project

Our music is more than just sound; it is our history written in the air. For generations, the Te B’atua (composers) of Kiribati have captured the spirit of the wind and sea.

Te Anene is a community-driven archive built to ensure the Te Mauri, Te Raoi ao Te Tabomoa is shared through the songs of our ancestors and the vibrant voices of today.

Our Commitment

"We respect the intellectual and spiritual property of every family and island. Every song tells a story that belongs to our people."
